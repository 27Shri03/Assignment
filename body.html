Who we are ?

We believe that electronic waste and lithium-ion batteries are among the most pressing environmental issues of our time. Electronic waste, or e-waste, is the fastest-growing waste stream in the world, and it contains toxic materials that can harm the environment and human health. Li batteries, while providing many benefits, also pose a risk due to their flammable nature and potential for hazardous materials to leach into the soil and waterways if not disposed of properly. Our goal is to educate and empower individuals, businesses, and organizations to take action and make a positive impact on the environment. We provide information and resources about responsible e-waste and Li battery disposal, recycling, and sustainable practices. We also promote community engagement, collaboration, and partnerships to raise awareness and promote change. We hope that our website inspires you to take action and make a difference in your community and the world. Thank you for joining us on this important mission.

Product and Services

There are variety of Services which our company provides:-

  • E-waste pickup and disposal service: You can offer a service to pick up and dispose of e-waste from households, businesses, and organizations.
  • Refurbished electronics: Refurbished electronics such as smartphones, tablets, and laptops can be sold on your website after they have been restored and tested.
  • E-waste recycling: You can offer e-waste recycling services that safely and responsibly dispose of electronics that can't be reused.
  • Data destruction: For people concerned about their data privacy, you can offer a service that securely erases data from old electronic devices before they are disposed of.
